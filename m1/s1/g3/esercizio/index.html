Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wowyour taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/ 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/ 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/ 4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more far the pan

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, far brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    • necessary)
  • 1 1/ 2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together ali the dough ingredients unti! the flour is fully absorbed.
